Penta (PNT) Trading on HADAX Postponed, to Open at 15:00, June 22

Wed, Jun 20, 2018

Dear all,

Due to HADAX system issues, Penta (PNT) trading which was originally scheduled to open 15:00, June 20(GMT+8) will be postponed to open at 15:00, June 22 (GMT+8). Withdrawals will be available from 14:30 June 23, 2018 (GMT+8). Sorry for the inconvenience caused.
